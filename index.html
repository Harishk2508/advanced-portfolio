My Journey into AI

My journey into the world of Artificial Intelligence began during my early college days at Kingston Engineering College, where I discovered the fascinating intersection of mathematics, programming, and real-world problem-solving.

As a Computer Science Engineering student with a CGPA of 8.6, I've been captivated by the potential of Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing to transform industries. From developing deep neural networks for medical diagnosis to fine-tuning large language models for healthcare applications, I've consistently pushed the boundaries of what's possible with AI.

My passion extends beyond academics into competitive programming and research. Having solved over 640+ LeetCode problems and won 10+ technical competitions, I believe in learning through both theoretical understanding and practical implementation. This approach has led me to complete 12 major projects and even co-author a research paper published in IJIRT on hybrid AI frameworks for medical applications.

Today, as an aspiring ML/NLP Engineer, I'm driven by the vision of creating intelligent systems that can understand, learn, and assist humans in solving complex challenges. Whether it's protecting privacy through PII detection systems or advancing medical AI through LLaMA fine-tuning, I'm committed to building technology that makes a meaningful impact.
